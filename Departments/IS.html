information system Defenation:-


An information system is an system that consists of people ,data records, and manual or non-manual processes ,and this system processes data and information in any system or it is a group of overlapping elements that work together to collect ,process,store and distribute available information on a subject in a systematic ways to support decision-marking and to support organization,control and analysis in the organization and build a clear current and future perception of the subject of research
