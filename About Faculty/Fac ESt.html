Faculty Establishmemt:-



The Faculty of Computers & Information, Fayoum University, was established under the presidential decree No 267 of 2006. .

IIn its session dated 9/3/2006, the Supreme Council of Universities (SCU) consented to begin the study in the faculty in the academic year 2006/2007.



And recently:-

Dr. Khaled Abdel Ghaffar, Minister of Higher Education and Scientific Research, accompanied by Dr. Ahmed Al-Ansari, Governor of Fayoum, Dr. Ahmed Jaber Shedid, President of Fayoum University, and Dr. Rania Abu Al-Saud, Acting Dean of the Faculty of Computers and Information, inaugurated the new building of the Faculty of Computers and Information, in the presence of a number of From faculty members, journalists and media professionals, on Saturday, December 26, 2020.



Dr. Ahmed Jaber Shedid stated that the building consists of 5 floors, a ground floor and a basement, and includes 3 terraces and 14 laboratories, and is located on an area of 1,500 square meters. Its construction began in May 2016 and was completed in June 2020 at a cost of about 40 million pounds.
