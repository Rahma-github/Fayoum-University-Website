Computer science Defenation:-

The specialization is called by several names such as "computer science", "computing science", and "computer science"; However, the most common name is "computer science". Computer science is the third most popular major, as most international students come from all over the world to study in the United States, due to the high salaries that graduates receive after graduation, with different jobs in the labor market, and in various sectors. Students must realize that deciding to study a computer science major is not easy, and this major requires devoting a lot of time to study, analysis, and thinking, and spending a lot of time on the computer. This is so that students can reach a deep understanding of their specialization and tasks in the future; Students should think carefully before they enter computer science seats.

In FCI Fayoum:-

The Department of Computer Sciences offers a wide variety of degrees and certificates for undergraduate and graduate students. Through these offerings, our students study the developments of computers as well as designing and measuring the degree of complexity of the algorithms used to solve real problems. Moreover, the Department focuses on applications of artificial intelligence in which computers simulate the behavior of man in processing information. Other areas, such as: arabization of the computer, fundamentals of structural programming, logical design, object oriented programming, formal languages & automata, numerical computing methods, logic programming, analysis and design of algorithms, software engineering, assembly language, modeling and simulation, compilers theory, image processing, computer networks, theory of computation, neural networks, distributed computing and computer safety techniques, are at the core of the department's educational focus.
